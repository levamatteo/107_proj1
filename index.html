The Doors To Success

By Matteo Leva

In 2017 women all around the world came out in support of the Me Too movement
and took their stand against men who abused their postitions of power with predatory intentions.
I dedidicate "the Doors to Success" to the women who had to unfortunately endure the noturious Harvey Weinstein.
To the victims Harvey represented a doorway to the career they had always wished for, a barrier, unmovable and ruthless.
Throughout the panels of this piece I show various snippits of Harvey's life
displaying some of the origins that made him the way he was: a product of a dysfunctional family, a bully and eventually a criminal.
Placing an unstable man such as Harvey in the position he was in ruined more lives than could be imagined,
all while his staff, co-workers, friends, and maybe even family stood by and watched. I've represented the bystanders in my piece through the "faceless man"
who never stares at the hands of the victims which reach out from the door trapped within the frames of Harvey's life.
